About Greece

Greece is a country located in southeastern Europe, known for its ancient history, beautiful islands, and delicious food.

The official language is Greek, and the currency is the Euro.

Fun Facts

  • Greece has over 2,000 islands, but only about 170 of them are inhabited.
  • The Olympic Games originated in Ancient Greece over 2,700 years ago.
  • Greece has one of the oldest and richest cultures in the world, with a history that dates back thousands of years.
